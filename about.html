Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. It uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

What does HIIT stand for?

HIIT stands for High-Intensity Interval Training. It's a training strategy that alternates short periods of intense anaerobic exercise with less intense recovery periods.

How do I use the custom interval timer?

Simply enter a comma-separated list of numbers for the duration of each interval in seconds. For example, `10,5,10` would set a timer for a 10-second interval, a 5-second interval, and then another 10-second interval.
